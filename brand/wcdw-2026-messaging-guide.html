Language guardrails

Say this

  • ✓ people who are blind or have low vision
  • ✓ Envision’s Dallas Foundation, with the apostrophe
  • ✓ Envision, Dallas Campus
  • ✓ operates across Wichita and the Dallas area
  • ✓ one primary CTA per asset
  • ✓ use, improve, plain action verbs
  • ✓ tie copy to Envision and North Texas impact

Never this

  • ✗ impaired, or the abbreviation BVI (retired)
  • ✗ em dashes, swap for a comma
  • ✗ the Zoo as the headline, lead with independence
  • ✗ Envision Dallas as an org or market identity
  • ✗ retired names: ECDC, Envision Xpress, College Success Program, Talent Network
  • ✗ pity or deficit framing: despite, less fortunate, suffers, victim, confined
  • ✗ click here, utilize, optimize
  • ✗ every dollar directly supports, unless approved

Voice & typography

  • Voice: warm, grounded, hopeful. Human first, never clinical. Center agency, not deficit.
  • Typeface: Montserrat everywhere, sentence case.
  • Headlines: ExtraBold (800).
  • Legibility: keep body text 12px or larger; high contrast on-screen type.
